How the Starter plan works — $29 per run

Think of it like a laundromat. You pay each time you bring your laundry. Come back next week, next month, or next year with a new bag of photos — that is another $29. Each purchase covers one complete run of the tool, and nothing more.

There is no ongoing access, no free return visits, and nothing stored on our end between runs. Once you find yourself coming back often enough that $7.99/month would cost you less, switching to Pro is easy — just choose it at checkout next time.

How the Pro plan works — $7.99 per month

Pro is a monthly subscription. Your billing date is set automatically to the day you first subscribe — for example, if you sign up on the 15th of the month, you will be charged on the 15th of every following month. You do not choose the date.

Everything is handled automatically. You do not need to do anything each month — the tool runs on your behalf. Cancel at any time through Gumroad. No penalty, no questions asked. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing month.

What Off The Cloud does not do

  • It does not connect to or log in to your Google account
  • It does not delete anything from Google Photos — that is always your choice
  • It does not upload your photos anywhere — everything stays on your computer
  • It requires a completed Google Takeout export to work — this must be done before running the tool
  • It cannot recover photos that were not included in your export

Refunds

By confirming the checkboxes above, you have confirmed your device is compatible and you understand what you are purchasing. For this reason, we are unable to offer refunds for incompatible devices or change of mind after the setup guide has been delivered.

If Off The Cloud does not run correctly on a supported Windows 10 or 11 device despite following the setup guide, contact us through the website. We will do our best to help. If the issue genuinely cannot be resolved, a refund will be considered.

Please note: we are a small team. We aim to respond to all support requests within 5–7 business days. We will always get back to you.

Your privacy

We collect your email address at purchase to send your setup guide and respond to support. We do not share it with anyone. Off The Cloud runs entirely on your computer — we have no access to your photos at any point.
